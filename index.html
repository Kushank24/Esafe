About Us

E-SAFE ENTERPRISES is a leading edged Company, manufacturing, exporting and supplying FIBRE GLASS WALL SUPPORT SINGLE & EXTENTION LADDERS, FIBRE GLASS SELF SUPPORT LADDERS, TOWER LADDERS, ELECTRICAL OPERATING, DISCHARGE RODS & HOT STICKS AND PLASTIC INJECTION MOULDED PRODUCTS , primarily fulfilling the vital customers needs of Industries and Domestic sector.


E-SAFE ENTERPRISES has competence to manufacture its products of any specifications thus enjoys a leadership position in all the markets that it operates in India and world over. Its products are used by variety of customers.

Message From the Director

We founded the company in 2007 with an aim to revolutionize the Plastic Industry by introducing products made from Fibre Glass. Years on Year companies liked our products and we were able to get a handful of market share.

Today, we supply our products to around 23 countries ranging from Saudi Arabia to Australia. We are onto the process of automation and 90% of our products manufacturing is automated.
