ESAFE Fibre Glass Heavy Duty Self Support Pull Stool


About the Product

E-SAFE fibre glass heavy duty self supported Pull Stool ES-PSHD having side runners of fibre glass channel section, 6” wide fibre glass anti skid steps on one side. Pull Stool provided with nonconductive Moulded anti skid Top, Fibre Glass Pulling Handle of 18” length properly fixed with the ladder and wheels on the rear side for easy movability. Injection Molded Shoes with thick anti skid treads. Suitable for Loading Capacity of 140 kg.

Available upto 6 feet height.
